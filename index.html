About us

Enterprise-Grade Technology Consulting

Trigen Technology Solutions Limited is a premier Ghanaian company established in April 2017 in Accra, Ghana. We are an innovative IT infrastructure and consulting service provider. Trigen guides enterprises in deploying resilient, scalable, and secure technology solutions to achieve strategic objectives. Our frameworks are meticulously designed to resolve operational bottlenecks, optimize performance, and significantly increase return on investment.

Scope of Business

Technology & Services

Strategic deployment is critical to realizing the full value of technology investments. Our seasoned engineering teams execute flawless installations of mission-critical IT infrastructure, from edge devices to core data centers. We provide comprehensive lifecycle support, ensuring maximum uptime, reduced latency, and robust system integrity.

  • System Commissioning & Integration
  • Infrastructure Auditing & Lifecycle Management
  • 24/7 Helpdesk & SLA-backed Support

We architect high-performance environments tailored to your specific organizational workflows. Our design philosophy prioritizes scalability, security, and interoperability, translating complex business requirements into tangible technical blueprints that drive competitive advantage.

  • Cloud Architecture & Hybrid Frameworks
  • Network Topology Design
  • Disaster Recovery & Continuity Planning

Advanced infrastructure demands an empowered workforce. Our capacity development programs are designed to upskill your internal teams, transferring crucial technical knowledge so your organization can confidently adopt and independently manage emerging technologies.

  • End-User Adoption Workshops
  • Specialized IT Administration Training
  • Operational Process Standardization

Seamless connectivity is the backbone of the modern enterprise. We deliver enterprise-grade telecommunication solutions that ensure high-bandwidth, low-latency communication across local geographies and global distributed networks.

  • Unified Communications (VoIP/UCaaS)
  • Structured Cabling & Fiber Optics
  • Secure Wide Area Networking (SD-WAN)

Offload the burden of day-to-day IT management to our expert teams. Through proactive monitoring, routine patching, and continuous optimization, we act as an extension of your IT department—allowing you to focus strictly on your core business objectives.

  • 24x7 Proactive Remote Monitoring
  • Cybersecurity & Endpoint Protection
  • Database & Storage Management
